About Us

We are founded by experienced tech leaders and industry veterans with more than 20 years of experience who saw the untapped potential in coding bootcamp graduates and the pain companies face with traditional hiring.

Our leadership has experience working in start ups, and in the corporate world, in Silicon Valley, and in Silcon Alley. Our experiences span working as Software Engineers, Business Analysts, and Project Managers in teams collaborating across multiple continents.

We bring our experience to bridge the gap between outstanding talent and innovative companies through a risk-free, human-centered approach. To eliminate the risk and inefficiency in tech hiring while creating opportunities for exceptional coding bootcamp graduates with a few years of experience in the industry to launch successful global careers. We believe exceptional software engineers are the foundation of transformative technology.

Our Vision

A world where every company has access to exceptional software engineering talent, and every talented engineer finds meaningful work that matches their skills and aspirations.
