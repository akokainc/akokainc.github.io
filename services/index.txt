# Our Services

## Comprehensive Risk-Free Hiring Solutions

At Akoka, we understand that every company has unique hiring needs. Whether you're looking for a single engineer to join your team or need to scale an entire engineering department, we offer flexible, risk-free solutions tailored to your specific requirements.

## Service Portfolio

### Contract-to-Hire (Our Flagship Service)

Our most popular offering eliminates hiring risk entirely. Engineers begin as contractors under our management, allowing you to evaluate their technical skills, work style, and cultural fit in real-world scenarios before making any long-term commitments.

**How It Works:**

1. We source and vet candidates based on your requirements
2. Selected engineer starts as our contractor on your projects
3. You work together for an agreed evaluation period (typically 3-6 months)
4. When you're confident it's the right fit, transition to full-time employment
5. If it's not working out, end the contract with just 2 weeks notice

**Perfect For:**

- Companies wanting to minimize hiring risk
- Teams that have struggled with cultural fit in the past
- Projects where you want to see results before committing
- Growing companies that need flexibility

### Direct Placement

For companies ready to hire immediately, we offer traditional placement services with our money-back guarantee. Our rigorous vetting process ensures you're only interviewing top-quality candidates.

**Key Benefits:**

- Pre-vetted candidates save interview time
- Cultural fit assessment reduces turnover risk
- 90-day replacement guarantee for peace of mind
- Streamlined process reduces time-to-hire

### Team Augmentation

Scale your engineering capacity quickly with multiple contractors working in coordination. Perfect for large projects, seasonal needs, or rapid growth phases.

**Advantages:**

- Multiple engineers start simultaneously
- Coordinated team integration
- Unified management and billing
- Flexible scaling based on project needs

### Project-Based Solutions

Complete project teams assembled specifically for your initiative. From MVP development to feature implementations, we provide end-to-end project delivery.

**Ideal For:**

- Defined project scope with clear deliverables
- Companies lacking specific technical expertise
- Time-sensitive initiatives requiring immediate scaling
- Pilot projects and proof-of-concepts

## Technical Expertise

Our engineers are proficient across the entire technology spectrum:

**Modern Web Development:**

- Frontend: React, Vue.js, Angular, TypeScript, modern CSS frameworks
- Backend: Node.js, Python, Java, Go, microservices architecture
- Full-stack: Complete application development from database to user interface

**Mobile & Cross-Platform:**

- React Native for cross-platform development
- Native iOS and Android development
- Progressive Web Apps (PWAs)

**Cloud & Infrastructure:**

- AWS, Azure, Google Cloud Platform
- Container orchestration with Docker and Kubernetes
- Infrastructure as Code with Terraform
- CI/CD pipeline development

**Data & Analytics:**

- Data pipeline development with Python and Spark
- ETL processes and data warehousing
- Analytics infrastructure and reporting

## Quality Assurance Process

Every engineer in our network has been rigorously evaluated through our comprehensive vetting process:

1. **Technical Assessment**: Hands-on coding challenges and system design exercises
2. **Communication Evaluation**: Assessment of collaboration and communication skills
3. **Cultural Adaptability**: Evaluation of fit with various company cultures
4. **Reference Verification**: Thorough background and reference checks
5. **Continuous Monitoring**: Ongoing performance tracking and feedback collection

## Pricing Philosophy

We believe in simple, transparent pricing with no hidden fees or long-term contracts:

- **Contract-to-Hire**: Pay only for work delivered during the contractor phase
- **Direct Placement**: Fixed fee upon successful hire with replacement guarantee
- **Volume Discounts**: Reduced rates for multiple simultaneous placements
- **No Lock-in**: Cancel contractor arrangements with just 2 weeks notice

## Success Metrics

We measure our success by your success:

- **95% Client Satisfaction Rate**: Our clients consistently rate their experience as excellent
- **85% Transition Rate**: Most contractors successfully transition to full-time roles
- **< 2 Week Average**: Time from initial contact to candidate presentation
- **90%+ Retention**: Engineers placed through us have exceptional retention rates

## Getting Started

Ready to experience risk-free hiring? Here's how to begin:

1. **Initial Consultation**: 30-minute call to understand your needs
2. **Requirements Definition**: Detailed specification of technical and cultural requirements
3. **Candidate Presentation**: We present 2-3 pre-qualified candidates
4. **Streamlined Interviews**: Efficient interview process with top candidates
5. **Rapid Start**: Selected engineer can start within days of decision

Every engagement begins with a thorough understanding of your unique needs, ensuring we deliver not just qualified engineers, but the right engineers for your specific situation.

Contact us today to discuss how our risk-free approach can solve your hiring challenges and accelerate your engineering team's growth.
